Additional programs for Xobbi PK8000

Examples of usage for Emulator (http://bashkiria-2m.narod.ru/ | Vesta):
1. Normal loading - MSX-Basic format compatible
EMU01_bas_SLALOM.cas
To run: LOAD"SLALOM",R

2. Fastest loading - Internal PK8000-Basic format
EMU01_bas_SLALOM_csave.cas
To run: CLOAD"SLALOM",R

3. Internal PK8000-Assembler (Binary) format
EMU01_hex_FIRE.cas
To run: BLOAD"FIRE",R

Notes 1:
	These files are not the games, but they are libraries of words
	for game EMU12_bas_FIELD_csave.cas :
	EMU12_hex_LB1.cas
	EMU12_hex_LB2.cas
	EMU12_hex_LB3.cas
	a) load game FIELD
	b) load one of the libraries, for example: BLOAD"LB1"
	c) F5 or type "RUN" to run Games FIELD

More information: http://pk8000.narod.ru
