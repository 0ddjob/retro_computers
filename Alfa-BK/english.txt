Alfa-BK (Альфа-БК)

Year:	≤1990
RAM:	32 kB
ROM:	2 kB
CPU:	i8080 1.7 MHz
Text:	64x25x2
Graph:	128x62x2
Group:	Radio 86RK

Alfa-BK is a Radio 86RK computer with 32 kB of RAM in a 3 mm thick steel case.
One case weighs 5 kg. This computer uses a simple keyboard. A couple of layers
of film are glued onto a sheet of textolite. The user presses the film against
the textolite with his finger and closes the contact. After 25 years, the
keyboard works clearly.
